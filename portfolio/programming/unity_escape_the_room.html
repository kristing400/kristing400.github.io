Escape the Room: Parallel Universes Experimental Game Design | Fall 2016

Collaboration with Jason Ma

This is a co-op escape the room game built in Unity. We wanted to explore a non-traditional gaming style, where players have to communicate with each other through speech in order to win. In this escape the room game, two players are stuck in the same room in a parallel universe. Each player is given a seperate laptop. At initial glance, it looks like the two players are stuck inn the same room. However, as they communicate they will realize that the rooms are slightly different. They need to work together, find clues in the room, and figure out with objects are the keys to escape from the room. Of course, the players are not allowed to look at each other's screens.

Every clue in the game requires the players to communicate. A clue in a room may also require a trigger from the other room. For example, in this gif, the player in the second room needs to look at the painting in his/her room for the painting in the first room to reveal a clue.
