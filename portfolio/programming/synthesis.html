Synthesis Electronic Media Studio II | Fall 2015

Programmed through Processing, a software and language for generating visual arts through code. I was inspired by Casey Reas, an artist who creates art through the randomness of computers without setting expected images. I liked the idea of using code to generate interactive art that creates different results through every play.

Hope you enjoy it! The instructions are on the top left corner of the piece.
